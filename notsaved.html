What is a onion readable name?
After the v3.21a onion update, there was an optional <readable_name> tag introduced. It allows you to specify a readable name of your .onion link. After a client visits your site, his browser will associate your readable name with the .onion link and display the readable name instead of the .onion link after the first visit.
